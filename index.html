My Story

Prologue

Technology has always played an important role in my life, but around this time I started to become interested in the world of software development. I began looking into the industry and potential education options.

The start of my developer journey had multiple options to choose from. Of the many available paths, I opted to attend a school in order to have a structured approach and to have an opportunity to meet like-minded people.
